IPOL special Issue on Reproducible Research in Mathematical Morphology and Discrete Geometry (TC18 Special Issue)

Scope and Deadlines

In link to the main topics of the TC18 with Discrete Geometry and Mathematical Morphology, the aim of this special issue is to encourage submissions of recent works presented during different events from the previous years (like ISMM, DGCI, IWCIA, RRPR, DGMM and others).

The tentative deadlines are the followings:

  • Deadline for sending title, abstract and code: 2021-05-15
  • Online demo construction from draft code: 2021-05-30
  • Manuscript submission: 2021-06-15
  • Reviews: 2021-07-30
  • Final publication: 2021-10-01
    For more details and updates you can contact us at: si_tc18_ipol@inria.fr
