IPOL special Issue on Reproducible Research in Mathematical Morphology and Discrete Geometry (TC18 Special Issue)

In link to the merge of Discrete Geometry and Mathematical Morphology inside the TC18, the aim of this special issue is to encourage submission of recent works presented during differents events from the previous years.
